# Homeopathy Materia Medica - Common Remedies

## Aconitum Napellus (Aconite)
- Mental Symptoms: Great anxiety, fear, and restlessness
- Physical Symptoms: Sudden onset, high fever with dry, hot skin
- Key Indications: Acute inflammations, croup, bronchitis

## Belladonna
- Mental Symptoms: Delirium, rage, hallucinations
- Physical Symptoms: Red face, hot head, dilated pupils
- Key Indications: Acute inflammations, scarlet fever

## Nux Vomica
- Mental Symptoms: Irritable, oversensitive, angry
- Physical Symptoms: Constipation, digestive issues
- Key Indications: Digestive disorders, hangovers

## Pulsatilla
- Mental Symptoms: Weepy, changeable mood
- Physical Symptoms: Thick discharges, thirstless
- Key Indications: Women's disorders, catarrhal conditions

## Arsenicum Album
- Mental Symptoms: Great anxiety, fear of death
- Physical Symptoms: Burning pains relieved by heat
- Key Indications: Food poisoning, anxiety disorders

## Sulphur
- Mental Symptoms: Selfish, philosophical
- Physical Symptoms: Burning soles, offensive discharges
- Key Indications: Chronic skin conditions

## Lycopodium
- Mental Symptoms: Anxious, cowardly
- Physical Symptoms: Bloating after eating
- Key Indications: Digestive disorders

# General Principles

## Law of Similars
"Like cures like" - A substance that causes symptoms in a healthy person can cure similar symptoms in a sick person.

## Minimum Dose
The smallest dose that can produce a therapeutic effect should be used.

## Individualization
Treatment should be tailored to the individual's unique symptoms and constitution.

# Remedy Indications by System

## Respiratory System
- Cough: Aconite, Belladonna, Bryonia
- Asthma: Arsenicum, Natrum Sulph, Sambucus
- Bronchitis: Hepar Sulph, Sulphur, Antimonium Tart

## Digestive System
- Constipation: Nux Vomica, Alumina, Bryonia
- Diarrhea: Arsenicum, Podophyllum, Sulphur
- Acid Reflux: Natrum Phos, Robinia, Nux Vomica

## Nervous System
- Anxiety: Aconite, Arsenicum, Gelsemium
- Depression: Sepia, Natrum Mur, Aurum
- Insomnia: Coffea, Nux Vomica, Belladonna

## Skin Conditions
- Eczema: Sulphur, Graphites, Mezereum
- Acne: Hepar Sulph, Pulsatilla, Silicea
